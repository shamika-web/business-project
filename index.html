About Us

Fresh. Local. Artisanal.

2+ Years of Excellence

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Mapusa, Goa, we are The Cheesecake Delight — dedicated to crafting the creamiest, most decadent cheesecakes that capture our coastal paradise spirit.

Sourcing fresh mangoes from local orchards, creamy coconuts from Goan shores, and premium ingredients from trusted suppliers, we bring together the best of local and global flavors.

From our cozy kitchen, we bake in small batches daily, ensuring perfection in every velvety bite. Whether you're grabbing a treat at the Mapusa market or ordering for your next beach party, we're here to make your cravings unforgettable.

Our Story

A Journey of Passion

Baked with Love

It all started in the bustling lanes of Mapusa, where Goa's summer sun and our grandmother's secret recipes inspired a dream — blending global cheesecake indulgence with local flair.

Tired of bland bakes, we experimented in our kitchen — infusing fresh king coconuts, Alphonso mangoes, and coastal flavors into every creation, until our first Mango Tiramisu wowed our family.

Today, we're proud to share our love for creamy, dreamy cheesecakes that taste like Goa on a plate.

"Every slice tells a story of tradition, passion, and the vibrant spirit of Goa"

— The Cheesecake Delight Family
